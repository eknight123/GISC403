Map of New Zealand showing 4 point markers that are important locations. Eve Knight - 73921084
This is a map showing 4 point markers which represent 4 key spots in of importance to me in New Zealand. The points of interest are seen as Hokitika, Christchurch, Auckland, and Geraldine. They also have the reason of interst within the pop up markers. The pink polygon represents another place of importance but symbolised in a different way. This also has the reason for importance in the pop up marker. The colours chosen have been chosen in a way that differentiates from the other layers so everything can be easily identified and uses the viewers assumption where neccessary. The underlying basemap was created in box map and correctly shows the labels and appropriate cartographic elements for the use of this map. The map has been appropriately scaled to highlight New Zealand and show the key points of interest.
